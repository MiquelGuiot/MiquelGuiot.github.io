Bones! I am Miquel Guiot Cusidó, a PhD student in Cryptography at the [Computer Engineering and Mathematics Department](https://www.deim.urv.cat/en/) of [Universitat Rovira i Virgili](https://www.urv.cat/en/), Tarragona (Catalonia), advised by [Oriol Farràs](https://sites.google.com/site/oriolfv/).

I started my PhD in January 2024. Before that, I was a Research Engineer at [Barcelona Supercomputing Center](https://www.bsc.es/) working in High-Performance Computing (HPC).

I obtained a MSc in Advanced Mathematics at [Universitat de Barcelona](https://web.ub.edu/en/home), Barcelona (Catalonia). Formerly, I completed a BSc in Mathematics and a BSc in Computer Science at the same university.
